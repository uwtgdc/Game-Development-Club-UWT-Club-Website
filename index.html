About the Game Development Club

Welcome to the Game Development Club at the University of Washington Tacoma! Our club is dedicated to fostering creativity and collaboration among aspiring game developers. Whether you are a designer, programmer, artist, or just passionate about games, there's a place for you here. We hold regular meetings to collaborate on projects together, as well as host game jams and other similar events to spark creativity and innovation among our members.

Join us to learn, build, and play. Together, let's create the future of gaming!
